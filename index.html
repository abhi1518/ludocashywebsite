FAQs

1. Is LudoCashy game safe?
LudoCashy has multiple checks and balances in place to enhance user safety and security. LudoCashy ensures that there is fair play in all its games and is a safe platform for its users to rely on.
2.Is LudoCashy a game of skill?
Yes, Ludo game requires demonstration of skills like dexterity, strategic thinking, logic, attention and superior knowledge of the game and hence qualifies as a game of skill.
3.What is the objective of the LudoCashy game ?
The objective of the LudoCashy game is to move pieces around the board to the home triangle.
4.Is LudoCashy Game on free ?
Yes, you can play LudoCashy Game on free and you can also choose to play at different boot amounts.
5.How to earn real money in a LudoCashy game?
Relive your childhood Ludo game but this make real money while doing it. Practice your game with free play option on LudoCashy app and then play with different boot amounts in order to make more money from the Ludo game.
